# Video Script Template (default)

> Embedded default. Override at `docs/marketing/video-roteiro.md`.

## 30–60s script frame

1. **Hook (0–3s)** — one sentence that stops the scroll.
2. **Problem (3–10s)** — who hurts and how, using the reader's words.
3. **Mechanism (10–40s)** — what we shipped, shown on screen.
4. **Outcome (40–55s)** — the concrete change for the viewer.
5. **CTA (55–60s)** — single action, spoken clearly.

## On-screen text rules

- Max 6 words per card.
- Always caption voiceover — sound off is the default.
