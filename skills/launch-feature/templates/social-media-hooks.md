# Social Media Hooks (default)

> Embedded default. Override at `docs/marketing/social-media-hooks.md`.

## Hook patterns

1. **Pain callout** — "Stop doing X. Start doing Y."
2. **Contrarian claim** — "Most teams do X. Here's why that's wrong."
3. **Before/After** — "Before: 3 hours. Now: 3 minutes."
4. **Concrete artifact** — "We shipped X today. Here's what it looks like."
5. **Cost framing** — "X used to cost us Y. Not anymore."

## Selection rules

- Pick the hook that matches the feature's primary user outcome.
- Avoid patterns 2 and 3 back-to-back — they tire the audience.
- Write two hook candidates and pick the one with the shorter first line.
