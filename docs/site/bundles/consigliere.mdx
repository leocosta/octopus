`consigliere` is a private chief-of-staff kit for an engineering manager. It turns
the firehose of inputs a manager swims in — meeting transcripts, Slack threads,
Jira tickets, Confluence pages — into a living, grounded memory you can track and
query. Where the `tech-lead` bundle multiplies the *team*, `consigliere`
multiplies the *manager*.

## What it solves

A manager's most valuable context — what was decided, who owns what, the political
read on a project, the pattern that keeps recurring — lives scattered across
transcripts, chats, and tickets, and decays the moment the meeting ends. There's
no good home for it: a team repo is the wrong audience and the wrong privacy
boundary. So it stays in the manager's head and erodes. This bundle gives that
context a private, structured, queryable home.

## How it works

- **Private workspace** — all managerial data lives in a private workspace, never
  a team repo, and is kept deliberately separate from the team-facing `tech-lead`
  bundle by audience, data, and activation context.
- **Digest, don't dump** — inputs are captured, routed to the right context, and
  distilled into a grounded, structured digest — then fanned out to the contexts
  and projects they touch.
- **Contexts and projects** — memory is organized along two axes: perennial
  *contexts* (a report, a stakeholder, a system) and cross-cutting *projects*.
- **Grounded and consultable** — the materialized state is queryable in natural
  language, read-only, and the heuristics that drive the manager's judgment are
  curated through a review loop.

## What's included

- `consigliere-bootstrap` — scaffolds the private workspace and its write-guard.
- `digest-source` — capture → route → grounded digest → fan-out.
- `context-status` — read-only natural-language consult over the state.
- `playbook-review` — curate the heuristics loop.
- `consigliere-connect-atlassian` — one-time read-only Jira + Confluence setup.
- `consigliere-lens` — reframe the knowledge engines through the manager's lens.
- `consigliere` — the role: the political-risk read and the heuristics voice.

## When to enable

When you're a manager who wants a private chief-of-staff that digests your
meetings and documents into living, grounded memory you can track and query —
separate from anything the team sees.
